Panel Gallery

Polycrystalline solar panels have blue-colored cells made of multiple silicon crystals melted together

Monocrystalline solar panels have black-colored solar cells made of a single silicon crystal, and they usually have a higher efficiency. However, these panels often come at a higher price.

Thin film panels are based on silicon and glass technology and have ROHS certification and are the most environmentally friendly on market. They are normally best in output in produced power / rated W in specifications. They are best in hot areas and can handle twilight and foggy weather better than crystaline panels
